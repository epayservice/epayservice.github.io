What is the advantage of OpenAPI?

OpenAPI allows banks and financial services to use their resources securely. Thanks to openness to the outside world, OpenAPI helps to find the best and most effective solutions.
  • Improved Security
  • Customer own their data
  • Easy integration
  • Accountability

Data protection

Before providing access to customer data, we require his/her approval. All data is protected and authorization is required to access it through ePayService OpenAPI. To authenticate users, we use security standards such as the OAuth2 protocol and OpenID Connect system.
