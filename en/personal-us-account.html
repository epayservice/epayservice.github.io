ACH and Domestic Wire bank transfers within the USA for everyone!

Own US Account Number

The payment's receiver and sender is yourself or your company
You can use the US bank account to conclude contracts with payers, because this account number belongs to yourself or your company.
Several US accounts
If your business needs multiple US Account Numbers to split money flows, it’s possible to get them in 1 ePayService account.
Fast clearing terms
ACH transfers between US banks are usually received within 1 business day, while classic wire transfers take from 1 to 3 days. Domestic US wire transfers are carried out directly, without the participation of correspondent banks. ePayService supports the Same-Day ACH transfer option.

Benefits of a personal US account

The US account number works for receiving and sending USD transfers within the United States. With this new ePayService option you get all the benefits of a US bank account.
  • The US account in the name of yourself or your company
  • Transfers received during 1 working day
  • Get paid in USD from the US companies
  • Transfers to all 50 US states
  • American ACH and domestic wire transfers in USD
  • International Wire Transfers in USD
